Greetings from Dr. Walker

Welcome to Quilted Rose Clinical Services where we specialize in personal and community transformation.

Hello, I am Dr. Lisa Walker, a psychologist and licensed professional counselor supervisor. I specialize in helping Black and Indigenous people navigate cultural trauma. Cultural trauma can occur from many sources: family, religion, employment, school, transracial adoption, medical / surgical / birthing… I can help you work through this pain somatically.
